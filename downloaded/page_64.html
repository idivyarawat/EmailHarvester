    Get to know AirPods.

    • Active Noise Cancellation

      Control what you hear. And what you don’t.

      Active Noise Cancellation

      Active Noise Cancellation reduces unwanted sounds so you can immerse yourself in what you’re listening to or simply stay focused. And Transparency mode lets you comfortably hear the world around you.

    • Personalized Spatial Audio

      Immersive sound. Fine‑tuned to you.

      Personalized Spatial Audio

      Personalized Spatial Audio2 brings you 3D sound suited to your specific ear shape for a theater-like experience that surrounds you with sound.

    • Hearing Health

      Check, aid, and help protect your hearing.1

      Hearing Health

      AirPods Pro 2 unlock the world’s first all-in-one hearing health experience: a scientifically validated Hearing Test,1 a clinical-grade Hearing Aid,1 and active Hearing Protection3 — available with a free software update this fall.

    • Siri

      Your always-on intelligent assistant.4

      Hey Siri,
      play
      something
      upbeat.

      Siri

      Control your audio, get directions, check the weather, and more with a simple “Siri”5 or “Hey Siri.” With Announce Notifications, Siri can automatically read important notifications as they arrive. And with Siri Interactions, you can nod your head yes or shake your head no when Siri asks if you’d like to hear a message, answer a call, or manage other notifications.6

    • Magical Experience

      Simply effortless.

      Magical Experience

      With magically effortless features like one-tap setup, Automatic Switching,7 Audio Sharing, and more, AirPods make the listening experience completely fluid — day to day, device to device.
