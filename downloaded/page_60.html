    on one magical device.

    Get to know iPad.

    • Productivity

      Your workplace
      can be any place.

      Productivity

      Your workplace
      can
      be any place.

      Versatility comes standard.

      Theres never been a more joyful way of getting stuff done than with iPad. Designed for Multi-Touch, simple gestures help you get around smoothly and quickly. Add an ApplePencil to write and draw your ideas or attach a keyboard to rip through your inbox. And do it all with the game-changing performance of Applesilicon.1

      Work from anywhere.

      Connect with family, friends, and your team wherever you go. Speedy Wi-Fi lets you download, upload, and transfer whatever documents, folders, and files you need from just about anywhere. And with 5G, you can stay connected even when youre away from Wi-Fi.2

      Store whats important. Get to it with ease.

      Organize, share, and get to all your stuff with the Files app. And easily access and manage whats on your iPad, in iCloud, or on cloud services like Box.

    • Creativity

      Take your inner artist
      out and about.

      Creativity

      Take your inner
      artist
      out and about.

      Your most creative canvas yet.

      With a fully immersive touchscreen, iPad is a canvas thats ready for you to express your most creative self. From 3D sculpting and composing a beat to painting a masterpiece in Adobe Fresco, iPad is where your most creative ideas come to life.

      ApplePencil is the ultimate creative tool.

      ApplePencil lets you easily sketch a new idea in Notes, finish a watercolor in Procreate, or draw up production-ready architectural plans in SketchUp.1

      Take your best shot. And video.

      iPad has powerful front and back cameras that allow you to create feature films, high-quality photos, and other amazing content. Apps like FinalCutPro and Adobe Lightroom let you shoot, edit, and share all from one portable device.

    • Learning

      Your classroom
      can be anywhere.

      Learning

      Your classroom can be anywhere.

      Learning untethered.

      With fast Wi-Fi and cellular capabilities, built-in cameras help you create powerful connections wherever you are. Join class, attend a lecture on Zoom, tackle a group project on FaceTime, or simply stay in touch with colleagues and classmates from allover.

      Enhance your education with accessories.

      Jump-start your notes and projects with accessories that were made for iPad.1 Grab an ApplePencil and take handwritten and digital notes or connect a keyboard and make quick work of all your emails, essays, andpresentations.

      Apps for discovery and exploration.

      iPad is perfect for learning something new. From step-by-step instructions with Kitchen Stories to zipping through a selfguided course, theres an app forwhatever and however you want to learn, including immersive augmented reality apps.

    • Entertainment

      Kick back. Tune in.
      Game on.

      Entertainment

      Kick back. Tune in. Game on.

      Always have your favorite content with you.

      iPad is a 24/7 all-access pass to your favorite movies, TV shows, books, and music wherever you are.

      A home theater in your hands.

      With an immersive display, amazing speakers, and advanced display technologies like ProMotion, all your content looks and sounds amazing.3

      Ready, set, game.

      The power of Applesilicon combined with a stunning display makes iPad the ultimate immersive gaming experience. Level up by attaching a keyboard or mouse. You can even connect a controller to create a portable console and play games like Call of Duty:Warzone Mobile.1

    • ApplePencil

      Dream it up.
      Jot it down.

      ApplePencil

      Dream it up. Jot it down.

      ApplePencil sets the standard for drawing, writing, and note-taking.

      Keep a journal, quickly jot down a grocery list, and take amazing class notes. Want to convert those notes to text? No problem. iPad is ready to capture every thought.

      Calling all artists.

      Whether its designing something beautiful in Procreate, brushing up your photos in Affinity Photo 2, or finishing your home project in Shapr3D, ApplePencil has the ability to bring your creative ideas tolife with incredible precision.

      Special features to help your workflow.

      With iPadOS, ApplePencil feels more like writing on paper. Easily sign documents, mark up screenshots, write in text fields, and even leave a note over an app or screen with QuickNotes.

    • iPadOS + Apps

      Everyday superpowers. Built right in.

      iPadOS + Apps

      Everyday superpowers. Built right in.

      Touch. And go.

      Designed for the large Multi-Touch display, iPadOS is the operating system that makes iPad so powerful and easy to use. It pushes the capabilities of iPad further all using the power of touch.

      Powerful and capable from the start.

      Essential Apple-designed apps like Keynote, Numbers, Pages, and more were created to take full advantage of the unique power and versatility of iPad.

      Over a million other ways to use it.

      Turn your iPad into almost anything from a math classroom or workstation to a professional recording studio with apps from the AppStore. With over a million apps made just for iPad, theres something for everyone.
