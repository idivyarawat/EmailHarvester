NIFTY50 and SENSEX ended higher for the second day in a row, Divi's Laboratories and LTIMindtree among top gainers

By Upstox News Desk

2 min read | Updated on September 10, 2024, 16:36 IST

SUMMARY

In the final phase of trading on Tuesday, profit booking was observed in the frontline indices, causing them to pull back from intraday highs. Despite this, the NIFTY50 index managed to close above the 25,000 mark. The broader markets outperformed, with both the Nifty Midcap 100 and Smallcap 100 indices gaining over 1%.

Benchmark indices close in the green second day in a row

The Indian benchmark indices ended higher for the second day in a row on Tuesday. Despite trimming nearly 90 points from the intraday high of 25,130.50, the NIFTY50 settled above the important level of 25,000 led by Bharti Airtel (+2.28%) and Infosys (+0.93%). The SENSEX closed above the 81,900 level, up by 0.44% or 361.75 points. The Nifty Bank closed above the level of 51,250 up by 154.50 points. The India VIX declined by 6.18% to 13.36.

The broader markets outperformed the frontline indices with Nifty Midcap 100 and Smallcap 100 index gaining over 1%. Considering the outperformance by the broader markets, the market breadth was strong for the day.

Majority of the sectoral indices ended in green led by Nifty Media, which gained about 2.7%. Nifty FMCG, which added 0.18%, closed at a record high.

Top Gainers & Losers

In the NIFTY50 space, 34 stocks advanced, and 16 stocks declined. Diviâs Lab (+5.23%), LTI Mindtree (+3.02%), NTPC (+2.39%), Bharti Airtel (+2.23%), and Wipro (+2.02%) were the top gainers. Laggards included HDFC Life (-4.40%), SBI Life (-2.46%), Shriram Finance (-1.80%), Bajaj Finserv (-1.75%), and Bajaj Finance (-1.41%).

Index Movers

Coming to the NIFTY50 index pullers and draggers, Bharti Airtel (+20.78 pts), Infosys (+15.88 pts), TCS (+13.81 pts), Axis Bank (+10.06 pts), and NTPC (+9.96 pts) were the pullers of the day. Draggers included HDFC Life (-7.7 pts), Bajaj Finance (-6.5 pts), SBI Life (-4.75 pts), Bajaj Finserv (-3.98 pts), and Hindustan Unilever (-3.93 pts).

Broader indices

Today, the broader markets ended the day in green. The Nifty Midcap 100 surged by +1.21%, and the Nifty Smallcap 100 index closed up by +1.18%. Market breadth was strong on Tuesday as the advancing stocks outnumbered the declining stocks.

Sectoral Indices

On Tuesday, most of the sectoral indices closed the day on a positive note. The top three gainers were Nifty Media (+2.66%), Nifty IT (+1.77%), and Nifty Pharma (+1.09%). The top three losers were Nifty Financial Services (-0.31%), Nifty Oil & Gas (-0.24%), and Nifty PSU Bank (-0.05%).

Asian Markets

The key Asian indices closed on a positive note today, barring the Kospi, which fell by -0.50%. The Shanghai index gained by +0.28%, and the Hang Seng index climbed by +0.22%.

As of 3:44 PM, The Dow Jones futures were trading down by -38 points at 40,844.
