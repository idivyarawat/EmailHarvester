    Description

    Have you run out of space on your device? Find out how Cleaner Pro removes similar photos and videos, screenshots, large videos ... in a few taps!

    What can Cleaner Pro do?

    OPTIMIZE YOUR PHONE & ICLOUD STORAGE

    - Delete similar photos and videos
    - Delete duplicated photos and videos
    - Delete large videos
    - Delete screenshots
    - Remove old calendar events
    In today's world where huge amounts of data are constantly circulating, we often want to delete unwanted photos that have accumulated on our cell phones. That's where Cleaner Pro come to your rescue! Our app allows you to search and delete screenshots, similar & duplicated images and videos, large videos.

    ORGANIZE YOUR LIFE

    - Remove old calendar events

    Tired of seeing multiple identical events in your calendar? Cleaner Pro is a real savior when it comes to keeping your calendar & reminders organized. You can delete past and completed items.

    OPTIMIZE YOUR DEVICE

    In addition to cleaning your device, Cleaner Pro also has articles to help you learn more about your device and help you manually delete content. Check out our Cleaning guides section to read tips and learn more.
