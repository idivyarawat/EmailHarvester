    for a healthy life.

    Get to know Apple Watch.

    • Health

      Knows you by heart.

      Health

      Knows you by heart.

      Never miss a beat.

      With the ECG app, Apple Watch is capable of generating an ECG similar to a single-lead electrocardiogram.2 And the Heart Rate app alerts you of high and low heart rates, as well as irregular rhythm.3

      You snooze. You win.

      Set up a bedtime routine and track your sleep. See the time you spent in REM, Core, and Deep sleep, as well as when you might have woken up. And coming this month, get notified if you show signs of possible sleep apnea.4

      Better understand your daily health status.

      The new Vitals app can help you identify changes in your health.5 Quickly see your key overnight health metrics right on your wrist — including heart rate, respiratory rate, wrist temperature,6 and sleep duration.

      The very picture of health.

      Apple Watch works with the Health app on iPhone to give you important insights on your sleep, activity, heart rate, menstrual cycle,7 and mental health.

    • Fitness

      Every move counts.

      Fitness

      Every move counts.

      Close your rings.

      Three rings show all the ways you’re active and help you reach your Move, Exercise, and Stand goals every day.

      Works for all your workouts.

      From cycling to HIIT to yoga, you have a healthy number of ways to work out — with accurate tracking of all the metrics you most want to see.

      Hit the ground running.

      Apple Watch has everything you need for running. You can use views like Heart Rate Zones, target specific time goals with Pacer, or do interval training with custom workouts.

      Go above, below, and beyond.

      Apple Watch is water resistant,8 dust resistant,9 and crack resistant. So you can swim, hike, and do everything in between without a worry.

    • Connectivity

      The right call for
      staying in touch.

      Connectivity

      The right call for staying in touch.

      Stay connected from your wrist.

      Call, text, get notifications, and listen to music from almost anywhere. With a cellular plan, you can do it all without your phone while jogging or running an errand.10

      Your wallet. Without the wallet.

      Pay for just about anything right from your wrist with Apple Pay,11 or send money to friends with Apple Cash.12 Use Wallet as your ID, home key, car key, boarding pass, and transit card.13

      Music and podcasts to your ears.

      Stream over 100 million songs with Apple Music.14 Download podcasts to your watch. Enjoy that new audiobook. And listen to it all on your AirPods or the built-in speaker on Apple Watch Series 10 or Apple Watch Ultra 2.

    • Safety

      Good help is
      easy to find.

      Safety

      Good help is easy to find.

      Emergency services when you need them.

      Apple Watch can detect if you take a hard fall or are in a severe crash and then automatically call for help, share your location, and notify your emergency contacts.15

      Peace of mind on your wrist.

      Check In notifies someone when you arrive at a destination. If you’re going for a run after dark, you can start a Check In for the duration of your workout to automatically notify a friend when you’re done.16

      Find My. What a find.

      The Find My app helps you easily find a friend in a crowded place or share your location to stay connected. You can also locate your lost or stolen devices.

      Sound the alarm.

      With Siren on Apple Watch Ultra, you can quickly alert those around you when you need help. Just press and hold the Action button and wait for the countdown.

    • Apple Watch + iPhone

      Dynamic duo.

      Apple Watch + iPhone

      Dynamic duo.

      Lost and pinged.

      Ping your nearby iPhone to hear where it’s hiding. Precision Finding can direct you to it by showing its approximate distance and direction.17

      Lights. Camera. Remote.

      Set up a group photo on iPhone, then use your watch to see and snap the shot or set a timer. It’s perfect for controlling your camera remotely.

      Turn-by-turn by wrist.

      Maps can provide turn-by-turn directions along with a haptic tap on your wrist when it’s time to turn. Great for when you’re walking on a busy street or biking a new route. You can even create a custom route on your iPhone and download it to your watch.

    • Personalization

      Make it you-nique.

      Personalization

      Make it you-nique.

      Rock your favorite bands.

      Watch bands come in a wide range of colors, styles, and materials, so it’s easy to switch up your look to match your mood.

      Dial up the faces.

      You can create an endless number of face combinations on Apple Watch — everything from Photos to Snoopy to Nike. Customize them, choose your complications, and even share with friends.

      App Store. Open to possibilities.

      The App Store on Apple Watch gives you access to tens of thousands of apps right on your wrist, like Waterllama and SwingVision. It lets you personalize your watch for whatever you love to do.

    • Apple Watch Ultra 2

      The ultimate sports and adventure watch.

      Apple Watch Ultra 2

      The ultimate sports and adventure watch.

      Strength in metrics.

      The larger screen of Apple Watch Ultra allows you to take in more data while you take on more. So whether you’re running, hiking, biking, diving, or exploring, it’s got your number.

      GPS far ahead of the curve.

      Apple Watch Ultra features dual-precision GPS — the most accurate GPS in a sports watch.18

      Go further. Go longer.

      Apple Watch Ultra gets up to 36 hours of normal use and up to 72 hours with low power settings enabled.19 More than enough to take on a marathon, long-form triathlon, or three-day hike.

      Action packed.

      The customizable Action button lets you quickly activate a variety of functions. Start or stop a workout, mark a segment, drop a Compass Waypoint, or begin a dive. To switch from one function to another, just press and hold.

    • Apple Watch For Your Kids

      Independence for them. Peace of mind for you.

      Apple Watch For Your Kids

      Independence for them.
      Peace of mind for you.

      A great family connection.

      Kids can enjoy all the fun, connectivity, and activity tracking of Apple Watch, even if they don’t have their own iPhone.20

      Learn more about Apple Watch For Your Kids

      Has everyone talking.

      Set up and manage watches for your children with your iPhone so they can make calls and send texts with their own phone number and Apple ID. You can even approve who they contact.

      Keep close watch.

      Your kids can share their location on Apple Watch, so you can receive alerts when they arrive at their destinations or get home.
